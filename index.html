Tableau Palette Creator

How to Use

  1. Set your palette name and type.
  2. Add an image that you would like to pick colors from. (Drag and drop, paste, or from file explorer)
  3. Click on the colors in the image you want to add to your color palette.
  4. Rearrange the picked colors if necessary.
  5. Click "Copy to Clipboard".
  6. Paste in your preferences and use!
